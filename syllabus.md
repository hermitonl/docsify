# HermitONL – The Minimal Path to Freedom

## Course-Wide Learning Objectives

- Understand the mechanisms of desire, attention, and modern cultural programming that create unnecessary suffering
- Develop a personal practice of deliberate withdrawal, minimal living, and self-mastery
- Integrate ancient philosophical wisdom with modern tools (Bitcoin, digital systems) to reclaim autonomy
- Cultivate a sustainable hermit practice oriented around presence, service, and continuous growth without dependency
